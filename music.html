Music

From an early age, Shaanze Noor had found a passion for music and since then it has only grown.



Choir

When Shannze was a little, they used to put on singing shows for their parents around the house and soon their parents noticed their singing talent. Shaanze was soon put into choir at the age of 7 in grade 2, where they continued to sing up until present day. They have always loved going to choir and it has been a huge part of their life which has also allowed them to be able to start creating their own songs.

Guitar & Creating Music

Just before the lockdown, Shaanze had been learning guitar in their guitar class. They started to learn their favourite songs on the guitar and soon realized that they could too try to make their own songs. When lockdown came around, it was the perfect opurtunity for them to start and so they did. They started out by writing lyrics about their own life and soon they made a few songs. They were able to find a creative outlet for themselves and a way to express themselves through music which had already been a big part of their life.

Music Tastes

Throughout their life music was very important, from their homelife to going to choir music was always around them which let them have a very wide understanding of what music was and lead them to what their music taste is now. Another factor that allowed them to explore so many different types of genres of music was their older brother who was the first person to introduce Shaanze into the dark and grungy music they listen to now. From scream rap to indie rock to even some classical pieces, Shaanze's music taste has a lot of variety and is definetely something that is more than just an interest.
